## Tape > opinion > DCF for short-term trades

For trades held <30 days, prioritize:
1. Price action (what the tape is doing)
2. Sentiment / catalysts (what's driving flow)
3. Valuation (DCF, multiples)

**Why it matters**: Starting with DCF and forcing the tape to fit leads to fighting trends. A stock can stay "overvalued" for months on momentum.

**How to apply**:
- Open every short-term analysis by describing the tape first (last 3 daily closes, volume profile, support/resistance)
- Then layer sentiment (recent news, sector mood, flow)
- Only then layer valuation
- For trades >60 days, valuation moves up the priority order
